Pipeable Operators: We already know Pipeable operators are the ones which take an observable as an input and returns another observable as a result without affecting the original observable(s).

Observables provide a method called pipe() which helps to use multiple operators together in a much readable and maintainable manner:

observable.pipe( operator1(), operator2(), operator3(), operator4() )

Based on their usage they are further classified into different categories: Join Creation Operators Mathematical and Aggregate Operators Transformation Operators Filterting Operators Error Handling Operators Conditional and Boolean Operators
